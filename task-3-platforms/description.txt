
For this task, I used Google Forms as part of Google Workspace to create a “Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz” for students. The form consists of five questions, including both multiple-choice and short answer types, covering topics such as digital safety, phishing, password security, and the importance of email etiquette.
After designing the form, I linked it to a response sheet using Google Sheets, which automatically records and organizes all responses. This feature makes it easy to analyze data and track participation efficiently. I also generated a shareable link to distribute the quiz among my peers.
This activity helped me understand how cloud-based tools enable real-time collaboration and data collection. It also showed how such tools can be used for academic purposes like surveys, quizzes, and feedback collection. In the future, I plan to use these tools for group projects, research work, and collecting insights, making my academic work more organized and efficient.
