## Phishing Attack Case Study

Phishing is a cybercrime where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information like passwords or bank details.

A common method involves fake emails that appear to be from banks or trusted platforms. The email asks the user to click a link and log in urgently.

Students and young users are often targeted due to lack of awareness. Once credentials are entered, attackers gain access to accounts and may steal money or data.

Consequences include financial loss, identity theft, and privacy breaches.

This highlights the importance of awareness and cautious online behavior.
